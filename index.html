ISKCON KUSHINAGAR

Hare Krishna ISKCON KUSHINAGAR is a Hare Krishna temple, community,ashram dedicated to the practice and prpogation of bhakti-yoga or loving service to Sri Krishna, the Supreme Person (God). We are a branch of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), a worldwide spiritual movement founded by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, in 1966.


About 53 km from Gorakhpur, Kushinagar is one of the principal centers of the Buddhist pilgrimage, is the place where Lord Buddha left his corporal self and attained Mahaparinirvana.
