Value Education Contest 2024



Value education contest is meant to enlighten the student community by creating strong urge with in them to systematically pursue education and training in the values for principle centred living.



Highlight Competitions:



Drawing Competition: Class 1st - 3rd



Article Writing: Class 4th & 5th



Shloka Recitation: Class 6th - 8th



There are Exciting Prizes for winners, 50 consolation prizes, and certificates for everyone.



Who can participate?


State Board / CBSE / IB /NCERT School Students from Standard 1st to 8th are eligible to participation in Value Education Contest 2024.
